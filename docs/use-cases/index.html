Use Cases

Crypto on-ramp​

Harbour customers can buy any supported crypto asset with their fiat currency. Through either Harbour's own UI, or an integrating app's UI, they will be given an approximate price quote, which varies depending on fluctuations in the asset pair and network fees, and payment instructions.

The customer will then simply execute a bank transfer to their Harbour magic bank account, with the given payment reference, and see their crypto wallet topped up within minutes.

As part of the Harbour API, the customer will be able to whitelist the crypto wallet address to which they wish to send the funds. Multiple wallet addresses are supported, each identified by a unique payment reference.

Crypto off-ramp​

Conversely, customers can also choose to sell crypto assets for fiat, by simply sending them from any of their whitelisted addresses to the Harbour Magic Ramp address, which is exposed via the Harbour UI or via the Harbour API for integrating apps.

The customer will set up a destination bank account through the UI/API, and will receive fiat in within minutes if it supports instant bank payments. The payment originates from their own Harbour Magic Bank Account.
